Art Smith's Sour Cream Waffle Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 tablespoons (1 stick) unsalted butter melted
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 3 large eggs
  • Maple syrup or jam, for serving
