Blackberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • 1 box Vanilla Wafers
  • 1/2 cup Pecans
  • 1 stick 1/2 Cup Butter, Melted
  • 1-1/2 teaspoon Vanilla
  • Filling
  • 3 packages 8 Ounce Cream Cheese
  • 1-1/2 cup Sugar
  • 4 whole Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Sour Cream
  • Topping
  • 2 cups Blackberries
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons Water
