Fried Fruit Pies

Ingredients

  • 3 cups All-purpose Flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 3 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Vegetable Shortening
  • 2 whole Eggs, Beaten In Separate Dishes
  • 1 cup Buttermilk
  • Canned Pie Filling Of Your Choice
  • Shortening Or Vegetable Oil For Frying
  • Powdered Sugar (for Sprinkling)
