Good Morning Muffins

Ingredients

  • Muffin Batter
  • 4 cups Flour
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons Baking Powder
  • 1/2 cup Shortening (can Use 1/4 Cup Shortening With 1/4 Cup Butter)
  • 2 cups Orange Marmalade
  • 1 cup Orange Juice
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla
  • 2 whole Eggs, Beaten
  • _____
  • Topping Ingredients
  • 3/4 cups Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon Nutmeg
  • 1 Tablespoon (plus 1 Teaspoon) Melted Butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • Wheat Germ (optional)
