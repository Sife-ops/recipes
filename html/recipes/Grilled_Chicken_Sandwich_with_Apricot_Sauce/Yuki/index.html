Grilled Chicken Sandwich with Apricot Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 whole Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts, Pounded To Even Out Thickness
  • Sliced Sourdough Or Other Crusty Bread
  • Apricot Preserves
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Cayenne Pepper (Optional)
  • Baby Spinach
  • Red Onion, Sliced Thinly
