Hyacinth’s Everything Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cup Butter
  • 1-1/2 cup Sugar
  • 2 cups Brown Sugar
  • 4 whole Eggs
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla
  • 4-1/4 cups Flour
  • 4 teaspoons Baking Soda
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1-1/3 cup Quick Oats
  • 3 cups Quaker Granola (oats And Honey, Yellow Box)
  • 1-1/2 cup Chopped Pecans
  • 1-1/2 cup Cran-raisins
  • 1-1/2 cup Apricots
