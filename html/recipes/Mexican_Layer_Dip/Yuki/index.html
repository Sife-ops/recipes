Mexican Layer Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 can Refried Beans
  • Tabasco Sauce, To Taste
  • 1 can Diced Green Chilies
  • Ground Cumin, to taste
  • 3/4 cups Grated Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 cup Sour Cream
  • 1 cup Guacamole
  • 3/4 cups Monterey Jack Cheese
  • 1 can Black Olives
  • 1 cup Pico De Gallo
