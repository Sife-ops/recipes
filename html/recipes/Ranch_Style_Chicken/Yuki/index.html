Ranch Style Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 cup Honey
  • 1/2 whole (juice Of) Lemon
  • 1/2 teaspoon Paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • Crushed Red Pepper (optional To Taste)
  • 6 whole Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • 1 pound Thick Cut Bacon
  • Bacon Grease
  • Sharp Cheddar Cheese, to taste
  • Canola Oil
