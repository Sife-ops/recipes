Sugar Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 3 cups All-purpose Flour
  • 2/3 cups Sugar
  • 5 teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 sticks Butter, Cold & Cut Into Pieces
  • 1-1/4 cup Heavy Cream (more If Needed)
  • 5 cups Powdered Sugar, Sifted
  • 1/2 cup Whole Milk
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • Dash Of Salt
