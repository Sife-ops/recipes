Wild Rice Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Wild Rice
  • 3 cups All-purpose OR (if You Have It) Cake Flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 Tablespoons Baking Powder
  • 4 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 3-1/2 cups Whole Milk
  • 2 whole Eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter, Melted
  • Butter, For Serving
  • Maple Or Pancake Syrup
